Who we are?

 Welcome to our About Us page, where you'll discover the heart and soul of our ministry. Here, you'll get to know the passionate individuals who make up our dedicated team and meet the inspiring pastors who lead us with vision and purpose.

Here’s the revised version without the original quote: Sacred Harvesters Global Ministry is a dynamic and thriving community dedicated to igniting a global movement of prayer and transformation. As the home of the Global Prayer Initiative, we are witnesses to God's boundless power and limitless possibilities. 

Through our journey, we have seen miracles unfold, heard divine whispers, and experienced the tangible presence of God.

Driven by a deep passion for serving God and loving one another, we are committed to filling our communities and the world with the presence and power of the Almighty. 

We firmly believe in the transformative power of God’s word and the undeniable impact of the supernatural in our daily lives. At Sacred Harvesters, we embrace a diverse, multi-ethnic membership, reaching across all nations, united in purpose and faith to bring about global change.

"Our mission is not just to preach the word, but to live it out every day, transforming lives through the love and power of God."

Pastor George Bassey
